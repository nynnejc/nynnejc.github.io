Nynne Just Christoffersen is a Berlin based web developer and art historian. She holds a masters degree in Decorative Arts Design History Material Culture from Bard Graduate Center in New York, and is currently working on a PhD in Art History as a fellow at Humboldt University in Berlin. She was recipient of the 2015 Rails Girls summer of Code fellowship. In her spare time she contributes to several open source projects and teaches Ruby on Rails classes at FrauenLoop.
