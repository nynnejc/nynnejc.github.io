Nynne Christoffersen

Hi, I'm a Copenhagen based software developer with a background in art and design history. I work as a fullstack developer at Siteimprove. I am a contributor to the tech collective Cybernauterne and one of the organisers with Et Ting. I also host discussions on tech and society in cph Tech & Science Book Club Sometimes I give talks. I teach a course at the IT university of Copenhagen called Architectures and Frameworks of the Web. I have been an active member of a number of communities in Copenhagen. I was the founding organiser of Rails Girls cph , the director of the board at Hack Your Future and one of the organisers of Google's Women Techmakers program in Copenhagen.
